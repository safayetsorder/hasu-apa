Welcome to Hasu Apa's World !

"Hasina Apa" or "Hasu Apa" is a term of endearment or familiarity often used in Bangladesh to refer to Sheikh Hasina, the current Prime Minister of Bangladesh. As the daughter of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, the founding leader of Bangladesh, Sheikh Hasina has played a significant role in the nation's politics and is a prominent figure in the Awami League, the country's leading political party.
